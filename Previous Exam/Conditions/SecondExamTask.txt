EXAM: UI Automation with Selenium WebDriver (05.08.2018) 

II. TOOLS QA - DATEPICKER
Create a test with Selenium WebDriver, which does the following:

1. Navigate to http://demoqa.com/;
2. Click on the "Home" page's "Datepicker" button;
3. Click on the "Datepicker" page's "Format date" section;
4. Select every option of the "Datepicker" page's "Format date" dropdown menu;
5. Click on the "Datepicker" page's "Date" input field web element;
6. Select "March 2018" as month option from the "Datepicker" page's pop-up window;
7. Select "1" as date option from the "Datepicker" page's pop-up window;
8. Verify that every "Format date" dropdown menu's option changes the value in the "Date" input field to its proper value. All possible 
values:
	• March 1, 2018
	• 2018-03-01
	• 1 Mar, 18
	• 1 March, 18
	• Thursday, 1 March, 2018
	• Day 1 of March in the year 2018
